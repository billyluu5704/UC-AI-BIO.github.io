Magnus Sieverding is a Ph.D candidate at the University of Cincinnati’s College of Engineering
and Applied Science. His work spans fuzzy-logic-based artificial intelligence, control theory,
dynamical modeling, and simulation of complex engineering systems. He assists Dr. Kelly Cohen in
projects involving validation, performance evaluation, safety assurance, and robustness analysis
of intelligent systems, with a particular focus on improving the scalability and applicability of
Fuzzy Logic to large, real-world engineering problems.
